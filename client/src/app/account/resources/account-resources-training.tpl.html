Q: What happens if another property finder has already submitted one of the properties I share with you?

A: Each property finder lead is time-stamped by our system. The property finder who shares the lead first gets the credit - first come, first served.

Q: This seems like a great opportunity, but what's my obligation here?

A: No real obligation - the time, energy, and financial commitment are completely up to you based on how motivated you are. As with anything, the more resources your willing to put in, the more you can typically expect to get in return.

Q: What happens if another team member has already submitted one of the properties that I have?

A: Each lead is time-stamped in our system, and the team member who submits the lead first will receive credit.
